Agile Credit is working to solve the multi-trillion dollar global Small Business (SMB) credit gap. The company building a financial services platform that leverages online data sources, including accounting, bank transaction & social data, to streamline SMB credit underwriting and relationship/loan portfolio management. The platform will deliver affordable credit products of $10K-$250K, as well as education and recommendations to help them manage their businesses more effectively.
