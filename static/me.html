About Me




Welcome to Aaron Cheng's Portfolio.

This is the place I share my thoughts, design works and useful tools. I am a designer with a wide range of interests including architectural, industrial and visual design. Currently I am focusing on UI/UX design. Having graduated from Rice University, I moved to San Francisco and worked with several startup companies on the user interface and website design. Design to me has much broader meaning than graphic, it is the combination of logic, technology and esthetics.

Everything is design.
