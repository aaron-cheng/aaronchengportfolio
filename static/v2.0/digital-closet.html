DIGITAL CLOSET

-  iphone App Mockup Design

 

 

 

10/2012

Digital closet is not a new idea anymore. Many firms have already started working on this kind of product. However, none of them is heading towards the right direction. In my opinion, a digital closet is not only a photo album keeps every dress as record. It could also be your fitting room, in which you can test which dress matches which pair of shoes and etc.

 

This design is trying to focus on the outfit instead of  single items. When user open this app, the first thing she sees will be a full-body model. By clicking the category icons above, user can choose the dress she wants to change. The secondary category allows users to sort their dresses by Brand, Color, Season and etc.
