CARLOS JIMENEZ STUDIO

-  Website Design

 

www.carlosjimenezstudio.com

Commissioned Work

2010

Carlos Jiménez is a famous architect and he was the jury member of the Pritzker Prize. (Nobel Prize for architecture) I was commissioned to design and maintain the website of his company.

 

When I went to his office, the first thing caught my attention was the wood building models he made. It is hard to believe that all the incredible details were crafted by hands. So I decided to show those models in his website design. Each web page has two parts: content on the top and a model photo at bottom. For the project page, the model photo is replaced by a bar of project icons. Visitors can drag the button below to scroll through all the projects he has done without navigating to another page.

 

Since this is a Flash website design, I took the advantage of Flash’s animation feature and added transition while navigating between two pages.
