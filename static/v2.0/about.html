ABOUT ME




Experienced UI/UX designer
I learnt to build my first website in 2010 and now I have been commissioned to design user interface and websites for several companies. For every project, I got involved in the overall process from concept to coding. I communicated with the clients and collaborated with their marketing teams to deliver the best user experience to the customers.

Problem Solver
All my design works are based on the process of finding and solving problems by creative methods, which has been the key for me to win five design competitions, including one golden prize and several other excellent prizes.

Generalist
I have design experience in a wide range of fields, including UI design, graphic design, product design and architecture design. Since most of the design skills are transformable, I can always benefit from my interdisciplinary experience.

Fast Learner
Learning is never a problem to me, especially software and computer. - Within two months, I learnt to use Cinema 4D making an animation for ‘Parking + Housing’ project. Now more than 16,000 people have watched it on YouTube and it has been published on over 10 websites.

PHILOSOPHY




To me, design is a practical process of finding and solving problems. I believe that everything should and could be designed better. The question is if we have the courage to break the rule that we have taken for granted.
