LocAZu

- UI & Branding Redesign

www.locazu.com


11/2012 - Present

LocAZu is the largest Canadian university and college student network connecting students to each other, and what a student need to get through school from textbooks to parties.

I am redesigning the UI and branding for LocAZu. Lack of hierarchy and poorly designed icons make the existing website graphically chaotic. Besides, the firm has very strict requirements of the layout and colour, which makes the design even more challenging. The first thing I did was redesigning every single icon and diagram in the website: make them bright, crispy and modern. To make the home page more attractive, I even designed three comic images to illustrate the frustrations students have in college. Then I used frames with black title block to unify the overall look of the website and clearly separated different contents.

The new website will be published soon.
