GOOGLE SEARCH SUGGESTIONS

-  Mockup Design

 

 

 

2012

This project shows some thoughts about Google Search:

 

1. The menu on the top left side is redundant because it is the same as the one on top. I suggest using ‘advanced search’ to replace it.

 

2. Currently, users have to scroll all the way down and click the tiny arrows to go to next or previous page. I suggest placing the arrows in the middle of the page at each side of the search results and making them much bigger.

 

3. For wide-screen monitor, half of the page is empty. I suggest adding second page to fully utilize the space.
